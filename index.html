ABOUT ME

Hello! My name is Angie and I am the author of this multidisciplinary portfolio.

I am a young and persevering artist graduated in Fine Arts in the city of Valencia. My specialty is painting, but I also develope my work in the fields of design and illustration. Due to the companies I have worked with, I've had the chance to continue with my artistic project while I've helped other workers to progress in their purposes. Do you want to share your aspiration and team up with me? If you need creative and profesional help, contact me! a.q.galiana@gmail.com
